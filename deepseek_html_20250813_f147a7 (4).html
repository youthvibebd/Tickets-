About The Band

13TH FUNERAL emerged from the shadows in 2020, a collective of musicians united by their passion for dark, atmospheric metal that blends crushing heaviness with haunting melodies.

With influences ranging from classic doom metal to modern progressive death, the band has carved out a unique sound that has earned them a dedicated following in the underground metal scene.

After two critically acclaimed EPs, the band released their debut full-length album "Nocturnal Rites" in 2022, followed by "Veil of Sorrow" in 2023. Their latest offering, "Eternal Shadows", represents their most ambitious work to date.

Known for their intense live performances and striking visual aesthetic, 13TH FUNERAL continues to push the boundaries of extreme music while staying true to the dark essence that defines their sound.
